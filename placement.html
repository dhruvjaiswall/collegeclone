Empowering Students for a Bright Future

The Training and Placement Cell at Government Engineering College Ajmer serves as a vital link between students and the professional world. We guide students through internships, aptitude preparation, interview skills, and company engagement, aiming to place every eligible student in leading organizations.

Placement Highlights

  • Highest package offered: ₹12 LPA (Off-Campus), ₹8 LPA (On-Campus).
  • Regular training sessions on coding, soft skills, and resume building.
  • Annual placement drives with multiple Tier-1 recruiters.
