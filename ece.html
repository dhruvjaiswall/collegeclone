About the Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering department at GECA focuses on imparting a strong foundation in electronics, embedded systems, signal processing, and communication technologies. The department offers practical exposure through well-equipped laboratories and encourages innovation in wireless, VLSI, and IoT systems.
