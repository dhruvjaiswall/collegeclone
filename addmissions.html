Welcome to Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page of Government Engineering College Ajmer. Here you’ll find all the essential information on eligibility, the admission process, important dates, and contact details to help you apply with ease.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Must have a valid score in JEE Main or state engineering entrance test.
  • Reservation policies as per government norms apply.

Admission Process

  • Step 1: Fill the online application form.
  • Step 2: Pay the required application fee.
  • Step 3: Attend counseling based on your exam score.
  • Step 4: Submit your documents for verification.
  • Step 5: Confirm seat and pay admission fees.
